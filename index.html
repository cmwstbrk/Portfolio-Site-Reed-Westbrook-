Reed Westbrook

Product Designer

Hi there! My name is Reed and I am a transgender/non-binary UX designer, family caretaker, nervous traveler, and lifelong learner determined to make the digital world more accessible, ethical, and inclusive.

Through research-driven design, I strive to create digital solutions that are inclusive, intuitive, and meaningful to all users. My commitment to accessibility and ethics ensures that everyone, regardless of physical ability or background, can benefit from the experiences I design.
